【遺失物效果】= Lost Item Effect

戰鬥勝利後，\n獲得的鐵道硬幣增加100%，持續5個任意月臺。=After winning a battle, increase the amount of Railroad Coins you receive by 100% for 5 platforms.
每有100枚鐵道硬幣，\n就使全體怪盜攻擊力提升5.5%。=For every 100 Railroad Coins you have, increase all party member's ATK by 5.5%.
每場戰鬥後，\n使當前攜帶的鐵道硬幣增加2%。=After each battle, increase the amount of Railroad Coins you have by 2%.
後續5個月臺無法獲得鐵道硬幣，然後攜帶的鐵道硬幣增加50%。=<size\=20>You will not be able to earn any Railroad Coins for the next 5 platforms, afterwards increase the amount of Railroad Coins you have by 50%.</size>
失去身上的所有鐵道硬幣，\n每失去100枚，全體怪盜造成的傷害提升10%。=<size\=25>Lose all Railroad Coins, incease all party member's DMG dealt by 1% for every 10 Railroad Coins you've lost.</size>
下一場戰鬥勝利後，\n一定會獲得1個橙色御守。=After winning the next battle, gain 1 Orange Omamori.
立刻獲得1 個隨機御守。=Immediately gain 1-2 Omamori at random.
戰鬥結束選擇御守時，\n增加一次可免費刷新的次數。=You can reroll once for free when choosing Omamori after battle.
戰鬥結束選擇御守時，\n增加一個選擇。=You have 1 additional Omamori to choose from after battle.
接下來的三場戰鬥後，\n隨機強化擁有的某個御守。=Upgrade a random Omamori after a battle for 3 times.
立刻隨機強化1-5個御守。=Immediately upgrade 1-5 Omamori at random.
獲得遺失物時，把1個已有御守隨機換成別的御守。=When you gain a lost item, randomly replace one of your existing Omamori with a different one.
立刻獲得60枚鐵道硬幣。=Gain 60 Railroad Coins immediately.
每場戰鬥後，\n恢復全體怪盜20%最大生命值。=Heal 20% of all party member's Max HP after every battle.
戰鬥失敗後不結束並視為勝利，所有怪盜恢復50%最大生命值。\n可生效一次，最終挑戰無效。=<size\=20>Losing a battle would not end you run, instead it would count as a victory and heals 50% of all party member's Max HP. This can only happens once and does not work on the final boss.</size>
獲得一個「勾玉」（戰鬥結束後復活1名不能戰鬥的同伴）。=Gain a <color\=#00A36C>Jade</color> (A consumable that revives a fallen teammate)
失去所有「勾玉」，並使後續無法獲得。造成的傷害提升20%。受到的傷害降低20%。=Increase DMG dealt by 20% and reduce DMG taken by 20%. You can no longer gain Jade and lose all Jade.
所有角色最大生命值降低50%，\n造成的傷害提升30%。=Reduce Max HP by 50% on all party members and increase their DMG dealt increased by 30%.
所有角色最大生命值降低50%，\n每回合回復20%自身最大生命。=Reduce Max HP by 50% on all party members and they heal 20% of their Max HP per turn.
戰鬥開始時，對己方角色生效：\n對隨機1個屬性變為吸，對隨機1個屬性變成弱。=At battle start, all party members gain Absorb and Weak on a random element.
戰鬥開始時，所有己方角色某項屬性提升30%。某項屬性降低15%。(攻擊力、防禦力、生命上限、暴擊率、暴擊效果）=<size\=20>At battle start, all party members increase a random attribute by 30% and reduce a random attribute by 15%. (ATK, DEF, Max HP, CRIT, CRIT DMG)</size>
戰鬥開始時，\n給所有敵人添加一個弱點，能被隨機我方怪盜克制。但是所有怪盜受到的傷害提升20%。=<size\=20>At battle start, all enemies gain a Weakness that can be covered by a party member, but increase DMG taken of all party members by 20%.</size>
每有一個不同職業的怪盜，\n進入戰鬥時，\n所有怪盜的弱點傷害提升10%。=Increase Weakness DMG by 10% for each different role your party member have.
造成的傷害提升25%，受到的傷害提升15%。集齊3張以上時，不會觸發負面效果。=<size\=25>Increase DMG dealt by 25% and DMG taken by 15%. Does not trigger the negative effect when 3 or more cards are collected.</size>
生命值提升25%，攻擊力降低15%。集齊3張以上時，不會觸發負面效果。=<size\=25>Increase HP by 25% and decrease ATK by 15%. Does not trigger the negative effect when 3 or more cards are collected.</size>
受到的傷害降低25%，造成的傷害降低15%。集齊3張以上時，不會觸發負面效果。=<size\=25>Reduce DMG taken by 25% and DMG dealt by 15%. Does not trigger the negative effect when 3 or more cards are collected.</size>
攻擊力提升25%，生命值降低15%。集齊3張以上時，不會觸發負面效果。=<size\=25>Increas ATK by 25% and decrease HP by 15%. Does not trigger the negative effect when 3 or more cards are collected.</size>
商店裡所有物品降價30%。但是每購買1個物品，漲價5%。（當前月臺不生效。）=<size\=20>All items in the store are discounted by 30%, but for every item purchased, the price will increase by 5%. (Not available for the current platform.)</size>
商店購買的第一個物品免費，僅1次。=The next item you buy is free. Works once.
在同一個商店中購買3件商品後，隨機獲得一個遺失物，僅1次。=After purchasing 3 items in the same store, get a random Lost Item once.
每次戰鬥勝利後使所有同伴攻擊力、防禦力提升5%。但是無法透過戰鬥獲得鐵道硬幣。=<size\=20>After winning a battle, increase all party member's ATK and DEF by 5%. You can no longer gain Railroad Coins after battle.</size>
每次進入一個殘影月臺，所有同伴的最大生命值提升5%。但是所有同伴的攻擊力降低10%。=<size\=25>After arriving at a platform with a shadow, increase all party member's Max HP by 5% but reduce their ATK by 10%.</size>
每進入一個月臺，獲得以下效果中的一個：獲得30枚鐵道硬幣；全員攻擊力+10%；全員恢復5%生命值；全員扣除50%生命值（最低扣除至1%）。=<size\=20>After arriving at a platform, gain one of the following effects: Gain 30 Railroad Coins; All allies gain 10% ATK; All allies heal 5% HP; All allies lose 50% HP (caps at 1%).</size>
接下來4個任意月臺內，受到的傷害提升30%。完成後獲得150枚鐵道硬幣。=For the next 4 platforms, increase DMG taken by 30%. Gain 150 Railroad Coins afterwards.
立刻獲得等於當前鐵道硬幣數量30%的鐵道硬幣。=Immediately gain Railroad Coins equal to 30% of the Railroad Coins you have.
接下來的2場戰鬥勝利後，失去御守選擇機會，直接額外獲得100枚鐵道硬幣。=For the next 2 battle rewards, gain 100 Railroad Coins instead of choosing Omamori.
獲得該遺失物時，每有1個「勾玉」，則獲得80枚鐵道硬幣。=Upon pick up, gain 80 Railroad Coins for each Jade you have.
